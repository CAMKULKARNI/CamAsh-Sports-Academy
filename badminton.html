badminton
court or lawn game played with a light long handled rackets and a shuttlecock volleyed over a net.the game is named after the residence of britain's duke of beaufort,where it supposedly originated in1873.officially sanctioned matches are played indoorsto protect the shuttlecock from winds.play consists entirely of hitting the shuttlecock back and forth without letting it touch the floor. head coach for badminton : master ashutosh joshi
